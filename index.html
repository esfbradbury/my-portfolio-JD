Jordan Delfino

IT Manager | Creative Professional | Frontman

Jordan Delfino is a seasoned IT leader with over 15 years of experience specializing in the high-stakes environment of international schools and luxury hospitality. Currently serving as the IT Manager at ESF Bradbury School since November 2023, Jordan oversees a comprehensive technical ecosystem, bridging front-end user needs with robust back-end support. Originally from Olongapo City, Philippines—home of the historic US Naval Base—Jordan has built a distinguished career through roles at prestigious institutions including the German Swiss International School and the Australian International School. His technical versatility spans Windows, Apple, and Chrome OS, complemented by specialized skills in CSS, web design, and professional multimedia production using Final Cut Pro and Logic Pro.

Beyond the server room, Jordan is a dynamic creative force in the Hong Kong arts scene. As the founder of Elephant Party Live Entertainment and the frontman for the band Jordan Road, he translates his technical precision into captivating live performances. Despite his professional accolades, Jordan remains grounded by his commitment to personal growth and his family; he is a proud father to two sons in Hong Kong and a daughter in Exeter, England, as well as a devoted grandfather to his six-year-old grandson. Whether he is on the basketball court, swimming, or performing on stage, Jordan lives by a simple, powerful mission: to be a better person every day and to provide unwavering support to those around him.
